> Andy Arant wasn't just another lawyer on the phone. He was a Water Valley public defender who'd stood by Matt years earlier, through the initial custody fight, long before any of this started — a mentor as much as counsel. By the fall of 2017, sitting in his family's condo on vacation in Destin, he was still taking Matt's calls, still reading drafts line by line, still willing to say the hard thing out loud. Andy has since passed away from cancer. What follows is, in a real sense, his voice on the record — a friend who told Matt the truth even when the truth was that no one in Oxford was willing to help.
